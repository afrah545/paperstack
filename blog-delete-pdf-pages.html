Is it safe to use?

Yes — and here's why it's actually safer than most online PDF tools. PaperStack runs entirely in your browser using the PDF-lib JavaScript library. When you load a file, it stays on your device. Nothing is sent to any server, so there's zero risk of your document being stored, indexed, or accessed by anyone else.

This matters a lot if your PDF contains contracts, medical records, bank statements, or anything else you wouldn't want to upload to an unknown third-party server.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. The tool works on any modern browser — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge — on both desktop and mobile. If you're on an iPhone or Android, just open PaperStack in your mobile browser and it works the same way.

Will deleting pages affect the rest of the document?

No. Only the pages you select are removed. The content, fonts, images, and formatting of every other page stay exactly the same. Page numbers won't auto-update though — if you need those renumbered after deleting, use the Page Numbers tool afterwards.
