⚠️ How this works
  • Pages you redact are permanently converted to flattened images — covered text cannot be selected, copied, or recovered.
  • Pages you don't touch stay as searchable text.
  • Metadata stripping (Author, Title, Creator, dates) is on by default — turn it off only if you need to preserve it.
  • Always verify the preview before downloading.
